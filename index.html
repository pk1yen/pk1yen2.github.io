The four services below are currently our most popular.

Two of our analysts will spend at least an hour working on your concept, and then send you all the notes, ideas, sketches, and diagrams they come up with! This will involve characters, related concepts, possible story arcs, structures, plot elements and more - enough for you to work from and take the story in any direction you want it to go in.

Two of our analysts will take your premise, characters, and any supplementary material, and give suggestions on how best to 'break' the story into a full plot. This will include structural advice, specific acts, character arcs, themes, plot elements, and other suggestions. We will also include links to supplementary material where possible.Breaking the story can often be the hardest part of writing a successful screenplay, and this service can help you avoid going down the wrong path, or making structural mistakes which have to be fixed later.

This is the service we recommend for most writers. One of our analysts will read your script and give you a succinct summary of what works and what doesn't. Focus will include premise, plot, structure, character, dialogue, pacing and formatting. Cheap and cheerful, this will let you know which (if any) major elements need work. If you decide to request a longer or subsequent report at a later date for the same script, you also get a discount!

Our 5-page coverage is our standard in-depth report. This is for writers who have progressed past their first draft, and want feedback on all the specific elements of their script in more detail. Focus will include premise, plot, structure, character, dialogue, pacing, formatting, writing and marketability, as well as industry scores for each. As always, if there is anything specific you wish us to focus on, let us know!

We pride ourselves on being more personal than regular coverage services. Use the form below to tell us where you are in the writing process, with as many details as possible, and we will advise you on which of our services may be of use.

We usually suggest beginning with the 1-page analysis from the LEVEL 1 section if you have a completed script. Many coverage services attempt to sell 5 or 10-page coverage right off the bat, sometimes even for very short scripts, but we think this can be irresponsible and unnecessary.

It is usually more cost-efficient to ensure that there are no obvious or large-scale problems with the script, so that these can be fixed before asking for more detailed development feedback. Many other services will lure you in to their longer reports, only to spend a large portion of the space commenting on elements which will have changed by the time these large issues have been fixed. An affordable 1-page report is therefore a good place to start.

If you are suffering from writer's block, our LEVEL 0 brainstorming and premise development service can be a huge help. We will work around your idea, teasing out further possibilities which can help you get over the hump (and needless to say - the ideas all remain entirely your own intellectual property, and a guarentee is given to that effect with every purchase of a LEVEL 0 service). This service in particular is one of our most useful, and can save you hours, days or even weeks of time!
