Lazy Poet Scripts is a unique, bespoke script and story coverage service
Providing a unique three-level solution to every writing problem you may have.

LEVEL 0

Brainstorming and premise development, story breaking.

LEVEL 1

Proof-reading, 1-page analysis, structural and overall coverage.

LEVEL 2

Deeper 5-10 page coverage, 1-hour Skype consultation, 1-4 week retainer.

We cater all our services to you as an individual.

Contact us using the form at the bottom of the page, or by email, telling us where you are in your writing process, and we will suggest the service which may help you most.

BRAINSTORMING

Two of our analysts will spend at least an hour working on your concept, and then send you all the notes, ideas, sketches, and diagrams they come up with! This will involve characters, related concepts, possible story arcs, structures, plot elements and more - enough for you to work from and take the story in any direction you want it to go in.
